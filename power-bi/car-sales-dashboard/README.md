# Candy Sales Profitability Dashboard

**Tool:** Power BI Desktop &nbsp;|&nbsp; **File:** `Candy_Sales.pbix`

## Problem

Revenue volume alone was masking a margin problem — high sales didn't always mean high profit.
Sales data was spread across systems with no integrated view connecting revenue to cost and
profitability by product, team, or region.

## What this dashboard does

A profitability-first Power BI report layering cost and margin analysis on top of standard sales
reporting:
- **Executive KPI bar** — total sales, profit, margin, shipments, boxes sold
- **Monthly YoY performance** comparison
- **Product-level profitability** ranking, flagging high- and negative-margin SKUs
- **Sales team and geographic** contribution analysis
- **Salesperson productivity** breakdown by region

## Key results

- **$141.49M** total sales, **57.3%** overall profit margin
- Flagged specific SKUs — including an **85% Dark Bars** line running at a **−35% margin** — that
  were high-volume but actively losing money, prompting a pricing review that a revenue-only report
  would have missed
- Benchmarked **4 sales teams** and 6 countries on both volume and profitability
